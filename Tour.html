    What happens on an Pac-Man Tour ?



    Walking--- Explore the city on foot and discover areas you may not find on your own. Let us know if you need a shorter or accessible route. Our routes usually include 5-15 minute walks between venues. Expect to walk 30-45 minutes over the whole tour.


    Stories--- Not just history but the story of the city through food and drink from the past right up to today.


    Reservations --- At four unique restaurants and pubs along your route. We take you to the places locals eat and drink.


    Eating --- A tasting menu of locally sourced food equivalent to lunch or dinner. Please advise us of dietary preferences.


    Drinking --- A tasting menu of locally sourced food equivalent to lunch or dinner. Please advise us of dietary preferences.


    Friendship --- Spend time with fellow foodies and your local guide sharing stories of past adventures and plans for the future.




    Why choose PAC-MAN route ?



    Locally Owned and Operated --- All the money you spend with us stays in the local economy supporting our restaurants, producers and guides.
